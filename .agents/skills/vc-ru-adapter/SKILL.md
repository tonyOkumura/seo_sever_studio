# vc.ru Adapter

Turn a full SEO article into a shorter vc.ru adaptation.

## Rules

- Public-facing language: Russian.
- Do not adapt until the full article exists.
- Do not copy the full article.
- The vc.ru version must be shorter than the original.
- Change the angle for vc.ru.
- Keep it useful as standalone content.
- Include a natural link to the full blog article.
- Do not invent facts, cases, metrics, reviews, clients, prices or timelines.
- Avoid clickbait and generic agency self-praise.

## Workflow

1. Read the full article and URL.
2. Choose a vc.ru angle: practical lesson, mistake, checklist, founder/business-owner perspective or comparison.
3. Create 3 headline options.
4. Write a short intro hook.
5. Condense the useful parts without copying the full structure.
6. Add a natural backlink sentence to the full article.
7. Add a soft CTA to Sever Studio.
8. Note what was omitted.

## Required Output

- 3 headline options
- short intro hook
- adapted structure
- shorter vc.ru text
- natural link to full article
- final CTA
- omitted details / TODO facts
