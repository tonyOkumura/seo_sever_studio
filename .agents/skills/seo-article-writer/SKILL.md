# SEO Article Writer

Write a full Sever Studio SEO article only after an approved brief exists.

## Context

- Public-facing content: Russian by default.
- Search focus: Yandex, Google Russia/CIS, local SEO.
- Business context: web studio / digital agency for websites, SEO, support, advertising, SMM, design, texts and audits.

## Rules

- Follow the approved brief.
- Do not invent facts, case studies, metrics, reviews, client names, prices or timelines.
- If a necessary detail is missing, write TODO or avoid the claim.
- Do not use generic AI intros or filler.
- Do not keyword-stuff.
- Do not promise guaranteed TOP rankings, leads, ROI or timelines without confirmed proof.
- Use short paragraphs and clear H1/H2/H3 headings.
- Include internal links, FAQ, CTA and metadata.

## Workflow

1. Read the approved brief.
2. Check confirmed facts and TODO facts.
3. Draft the article in Russian.
4. Add a direct answer block near the top.
5. Cover semantic entities naturally.
6. Include service-specific process, comparisons or checklists where useful.
7. Add "Как Sever Studio может помочь" with a confirmed service CTA.
8. Add FAQ answers in 2-4 sentences.
9. Add meta title and meta description.
10. Self-review for fake claims, overpromising and keyword stuffing.

## Required Article Elements

- search intent
- target keyword
- semantic entities
- H1/H2/H3
- short paragraphs
- FAQ
- internal links
- CTA to Sever Studio
- meta title
- meta description
