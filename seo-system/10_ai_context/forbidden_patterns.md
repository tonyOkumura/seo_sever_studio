# Forbidden Patterns

Do not use these patterns in Sever Studio SEO content:

- "In today's digital world"
- "game-changer"
- "revolutionary"
- fake statistics
- fake client results
- keyword stuffing
- generic AI paragraphs
- overpromising
- copied competitor structure without adaptation

## Also Avoid

- "в современном цифровом мире"
- "революционное решение"
- "выведем в ТОП гарантированно"
- "увеличим продажи в X раз" without verified proof
- "лучшее агентство" unless verified
- "под ключ за 1 день" unless confirmed
- "cutting-edge" unless there is a specific reason
- "world-class" unless verified
- "best-in-class" unless verified
- "seamless" as empty filler
- "unlock your potential"
- "skyrocket"
- "guaranteed"
- "10x" claims without verified proof
- invented founder quotes
- invented customer pain points presented as research

## Replacement Principles

Instead of hype, use:

- clear explanation
- practical tradeoffs
- real process details
- verified examples
- source-marked public facts
- TODO markers for missing facts
