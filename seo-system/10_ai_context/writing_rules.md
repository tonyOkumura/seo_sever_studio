# Writing Rules

## Language

- Public-facing SEO articles must be written in Russian by default.
- vc.ru adaptations must be written in Russian.
- Internal planning files may be written in Russian.
- English is used only when explicitly requested.

## Style

- Clear and practical.
- Useful for local business owners and SMB decision-makers.
- Not too corporate.
- Short paragraphs.
- Specific explanations.
- Calm, direct and helpful.
- No hype.
- No fake specificity.
- No generic AI introductions.

## Structure

Use:

- clear H1
- descriptive H2/H3 sections
- short intro
- direct answer block
- practical process
- comparisons where useful
- examples only when real or clearly hypothetical
- common mistakes
- FAQ
- conclusion
- CTA
- metadata

## Claims

Allowed:

- general educational statements
- clearly labeled hypothetical examples
- confirmed Sever Studio facts
- verified external references
- public site facts marked in internal notes as `source: public site`

Not allowed:

- fake metrics
- fake case studies
- fake reviews
- fake client names
- unsupported claims
- invented prices
- invented timelines
- copied competitor examples
- guaranteed TOP positions or sales without approved proof

## Editing Checklist

- Is the article useful without the CTA?
- Does it answer the search intent?
- Does it match Yandex/local SEO intent where relevant?
- Are the headings clear?
- Are paragraphs short?
- Are company details verified?
- Are all missing facts marked as TODO?
- Is the CTA natural?
- Is the metadata complete?
- Are links pointing to relevant Sever Studio pages?
