# Next Steps After Analysis

## 1. What Changed

The SEO/GEO system has been rebuilt from an English-first SaaS/MVP hypothesis into a Russian-first local SEO system for Sever Studio as a web studio / digital agency. The new foundation focuses on website development, SEO, site support, advertising, SMM, design, copywriting, audits and local commercial demand in Moscow, Moscow Region, Krasnogorsk and Mytishchi.

## 2. Why The Old Strategy Was Not Aligned

The previous strategy emphasized AI Product Design, MVP Development, SaaS UX and startup product strategy. The public site does not support that as the main positioning. Public pages confirm website development, design, brand, online marketing, SEO and SMM as the core offer set. source: public site.

Old AI/SaaS/MVP topics should stay only as archive/future topics unless they connect to existing public materials about AI, SEO or design.

## 3. New SEO Strategy

- Public-facing content language: Russian by default.
- Primary search context: Yandex, Google Russia/CIS, local SEO.
- Core commercial pages: site development, landing pages, corporate sites, internet stores, SEO, support, audits, Yandex Direct, branding/design.
- Content model: service page improvements first, then pillar blog pages, then cluster articles, FAQ/GEO blocks, vc.ru adaptations and internal linking.
- Local model: build useful location-aware content for Krasnogorsk, Moscow, Moscow Region and Mytishchi without doorway pages or duplicated text.
- Trust model: use only confirmed facts, public proof and TODO placeholders where proof is missing.

## 10. Risks And What Not To Do

- Do not write public articles from unverified service facts.
- Do not claim guarantees, top positions, exact lead growth or ROI without approved proof.
- Do not create doorway-like local pages with duplicated content.
- Do not treat Instagram/Facebook/Google Ads as high priority until admins confirm current relevance.
- Do not copy competitor article structure or wording.
- Do not keep English-first prompts for Russian local SEO work.
- Do not hide missing information; write TODO.
