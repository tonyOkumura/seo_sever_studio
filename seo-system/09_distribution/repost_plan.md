# Repost Plan

Use this workflow to adapt one Sever Studio SEO article into several distribution assets without duplicating the full article.

Public-facing language: Russian by default.

## Source Article

Before reposting, prepare:

- final article URL
- target keyword
- search intent
- core argument
- 3-5 strongest takeaways
- confirmed Sever Studio facts
- approved CTA
- visual asset, if available

## vc.ru Adaptation

Goal:

- Turn the article into a shorter Russian editorial post with a changed angle.

Rules:

- Make it shorter than the original.
- Keep the strongest practical idea.
- Remove heavy SEO sections.
- Link back naturally to the full article.
- Do not copy the full article.
- Do not invent local examples, metrics, case studies or client names.

Suggested structure:

- 3 headline options
- short hook
- practical problem
- 3-5 sections
- useful takeaways
- link to full article
- soft CTA

## LinkedIn Post

Goal:

- Turn the article into a concise expert post for business owners, marketers, founders or managers.

Suggested formats:

- checklist
- mistake list
- short comparison
- practical observation
- service decision framework

Rules:

- Keep one idea per post.
- Use plain language.
- Include a link to the full article if appropriate.
- Avoid exaggerated claims.

## Reddit Post

Goal:

- Share a useful, discussion-friendly version only in relevant communities where it is allowed.

Rules:

- Follow each community's rules.
- Do not post like an ad.
- Lead with a practical problem or question.
- Share useful takeaways directly in the post.
- Link only when allowed and genuinely useful.
- Be transparent if representing Sever Studio.

## Repost Sequence

1. Publish the full article on Sever Studio.
2. Wait until the URL is final.
3. Create a shorter vc.ru version with a backlink.
4. Create 2-3 social post angles from the same article.
5. Create one discussion version only if a relevant community allows it.
6. Track all links and performance notes in `08_tracking/`.

## Repurposing Checklist

- The repost is shorter or meaningfully adapted.
- The full article link is included where required.
- No fake facts were added.
- The CTA fits the platform.
- The post is useful even without clicking the link.
- The source article is recorded in tracking files.
