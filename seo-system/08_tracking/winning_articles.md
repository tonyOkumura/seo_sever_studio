# Winning Articles

Use this file to record articles that show early or sustained performance.

## What Counts As A Winning Article

An article may be considered a winner when it shows one or more of these signals:

- indexed quickly
- receives impressions for relevant queries
- starts ranking for target or related keywords
- gets organic clicks
- earns qualified leads or CTA clicks
- performs well on vc.ru, LinkedIn, Reddit, or another distribution channel
- becomes a useful internal link target
