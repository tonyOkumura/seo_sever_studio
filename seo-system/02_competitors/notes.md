# Competitor And Reference Notes

This folder is for research notes, not copying.

## Rules

- Do not copy competitor article structure directly.
- Do not translate competitor articles.
- Do not reuse their examples as if they belong to Sever Studio.
- Use competitor research only to understand SERP expectations, topic gaps, content format, and semantic coverage.
- Always create original outlines for Sever Studio.

## What To Analyze

For each competitor or reference site, document:

- main service positioning
- recurring topic clusters
- content formats
- article depth
- internal linking patterns
- CTAs
- visual assets
- FAQ usage
- author or company credibility signals
- pages that appear in search for target keywords

## What To Avoid

- copying headings one by one
- rewriting paragraphs with synonyms
- using their client names
- using their case-study results
- repeating unverified claims
- assuming their positioning fits Sever Studio
